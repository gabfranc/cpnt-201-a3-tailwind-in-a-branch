    • HELLO TO ALL!

    Welcome to ChocoPhili, the premier chocolate factory nestled in the heart of Quezon City, Manila, Philippines.
    Our legacy dates back to the late 1960s, when our business first emerged. Over the years, we've proudly preserved
    our heritage while embracing innovation. In the 2010s, we introduced some of our most modern recipes, seamlessly
    blending contemporary tastes with timeless traditions. As you explore our factory, you'll witness firsthand
    how we keep the old traditions alive while delighting visitors with our innovative creations.
